About Us

EDGE Engineering Solutions Pvt Ltd., started in the year 2009 to serve Geotechnical Investigation, Non-Destructive testing and Construction Works.

EDGE Engineering Solutions Pvt Ltd., has been rebranded as EDGE2 Engineering Solutions India Pvt Ltd., in the year 2021 and expanded its services in the field of Construction Material Testing, Design Review, Restoration, Rehabilitation Works, Project Management Consultancy and Quality Assurance with an own premises of 4 storied building consists of area around 5500 sqft. in Bengaluru.

EDGE2 Engineering Solutions India Pvt Ltd has a fully equipped Material Testing Laboratory with modern, scientific, and High-end equipments in the field of Construction material testing & Non-Destructive Testing (NDT).

We love to work in close partnership with our clients that helps us understanding their exact needs and thereby provide
independent Technical, Technological, strategic, commercial advice to deliver an all- embracing projects for the communities.
