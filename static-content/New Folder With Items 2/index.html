About Us

In 2009, Edge Engineering Solutions Pvt. Ltd. was established with the aim of providing services in Geotechnical Investigation, Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), and Construction Materials Testing.

In 2021, the company underwent a transformation and rebranded as Edge2 Engineering Solutions India Pvt. Ltd. It not only changed its name but also expanded its service portfolio within the realm of Construction Material Testing, Design Review, Non-Destructive Testing (NDT), Restoration, Rehabilitation Works, Project Management Consultancy, third-party inspection, and Quality Assurance. The company now operates from its self-owned four-story building spanning approximately 5500 square feet in Bengaluru.
