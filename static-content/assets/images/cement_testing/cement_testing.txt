CEMENT & CEMENTITIOUS MATERIAL
In our laboratory, we have top-of-the-line equipment and adhere to the specified environmental conditions outlined in Indian Standards for testing various cement and cementitious materials, including Fly ash, GGBS, Silica, and Micro Silica. Below, you will find a list of the tests we perform on these materials.
	•	Normal or Standard Consistency
	•	Compressive Strength
	•	Density
	•	Initial Setting Time Final Setting Time
	•	Fineness by Dry Sieving
	•	Fineness - Blaine's Air Permeability Method
	•	Soundness
