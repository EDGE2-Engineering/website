AGGREGATE TEST
Aggregates are tested for strength, Toughness, Hardness, Shape and Water Absorption to decide the suitability for all types of Constructions.
Some of the tests conducted on Coarse and Fine aggregates as per Indian Standards are mentioned below
	•	Sieve Analysis
	•	Bulk Density (Loose &Compacted)
	•	Materials finer than 75micron
	•	Specific Gravity & Water Absorption
	•	Flakiness & Elongation Index
	•	10% Fines Value
	•	Aggregate Crushing Value
	•	Aggregate Impact Value
	•	Bulking
	•	Los-Angeles Abrasion Test
