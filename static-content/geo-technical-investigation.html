Geotechnical Investigation

Precise Field Investigations

Our field investigations are conducted with meticulous precision, utilizing various borehole methods, including Auger Boring and Rotary/Hydraulic drilling. These techniques allow us to gather vital subsurface data, ensuring the accuracy and reliability of our assessments.

In-House Laboratory Testing

Once samples are collected from our investigations, they undergo rigorous testing within our state-of-the-art, computerized soil testing laboratory. Equipped with automated instruments, we meticulously analyze soil and rock samples to obtain crucial index and engineering properties. Our laboratory is committed to delivering results that adhere to the highest industry standards.

Advanced Cone Penetration Testing

we offer a wide range of cone penetration tests (CPT, DCPT, SCPT) to delve deeper into the subsurface characteristics. These tests provide invaluable insights into soil and rock behavior, aiding in informed decision-making for your projects.

Specialized Rock Testing

Our expertise extends to the testing of weak rock cores and broken rock lumps. Through point load index tests, we evaluate the strength and integrity of rock formations, ensuring you have a thorough understanding of the geological conditions in your project area.

Comprehensive Soil Testing

We perform compaction and CBR (California Bearing Ratio) tests on soils and sub-grade materials. These tests are essential for the evaluation of sub-grades, sub-bases, earthen dams, and overall soil quality, offering precise data to support your construction endeavors.

Ground Improvement Techniques

For projects requiring ground improvement, we specialize in innovative methods such as Sand Drains, Lime Columns, and Stone Columns. These techniques are designed to enhance soil stability and load-bearing capacity, contributing to the long-term success of your infrastructure projects.

Field Density Testing

In addition to our laboratory-based testing, we provide on-site field density tests of soil. These tests ensure that your project adheres to the required compaction standards, guaranteeing the durability and safety of your constructed structures.
