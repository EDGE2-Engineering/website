Precision Steel Testing

At our company, our commitment to excellence is exemplified by our state-of-the-art Steel Testing Laboratory. We take pride in equipping our laboratory with the finest tools to ensure the accuracy and reliability of every steel test we conduct.

Unleashing the Power of Precision: Our laboratory proudly features the renowned FIE Electronic Universal Testing Machine, a pinnacle of cutting-edge engineering designed to push the boundaries of material testing. With an impressive load capacity of 1200 kN, this advanced machine empowers us to examine steel materials with unrivaled precision.

Meeting and Exceeding Industry Standards: The FIE Electronic Universal Testing Machine at our laboratory isn't just any equipment – it's a testament to our commitment to adhering to the highest quality standards. Complying with Grade 'A' of BS: 1610:Part1:1992 and class 1 of IS-1828-Part1:1991, our testing machine sets the bar for accuracy, reliability, and credibility.

Explore the Spectrum of Testing: Within the walls of our Steel Testing Laboratory, we conduct a range of tests on the physical properties of steel that span the gamut of structural analysis. Our expertise encompasses the following:

  • Tensile Tests: Unveil the inherent strength of steel with our meticulous tensile tests. We subject steel specimens to controlled forces, unveiling their breaking points and load-bearing capabilities.
  • Bend-Rebend Tests: Our precision testing delves into the flexibility and durability of steel materials. We assess how steel responds to bending and rebending, crucial insights for applications demanding resilience.

Beyond Results, We Deliver Confidence: Every steel test we conduct is more than just a technical assessment – it's a pledge to uphold the integrity of your projects. Our meticulous methodologies and unwavering commitment ensure that the steel materials you rely on meet and exceed the most stringent quality standards.

Partner with Our Company for Steel Assurance: Whether it's skyscrapers reaching for the heavens or bridges spanning vast distances, the strength of your construction materials defines the success of your projects. Partner with our company to infuse your endeavors with the confidence that comes from precise and reliable steel testing. Contact us today to embark on a journey of engineering excellence.
