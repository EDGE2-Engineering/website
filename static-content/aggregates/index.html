Aggregates Testing

Aggregates undergo testing for strength, toughness, hardness, shape, and water absorption to determine their suitability for various construction purposes. Below, you'll find a list of some of the aggregates tests performed on both coarse and fine aggregates in accordance with Indian Standards.

  • Sieve Analysis
  • Bulk Density (Loose &Compacted)
  • Materials finer than 75 micron
  • Specific Gravity & Water Absorption
  • Flakiness & Elongation Index
  • 10% Fines Value
  • Aggregate Crushing Value
  • Aggregate Impact Value
  • Bulking
  • Los-Angeles Abrasion Test
